About

Since first encountering the troopers of the UK Garrison back in 2005 I have longed to become a Stormtrooper. In the spring of 2016 the dream became reality when I built my own armour and joined the UK Garrison and 501st Legion.

As part of the UK Garrison I now use my trooper to help raise money for charity and bring enjoyment to fans old and young.

If you are interested in becoming a Stormtrooper then head over to the UK Garrison or 501st (if you're not in the UK) for guidance.
